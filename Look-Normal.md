# Look Normal

* 2 oz White Rum
* 0.75 oz Lime Juice
* 2 tsp Absinthe
* 1 tsp Maraschino
* 1 oz Orgeat
* 1 dash Orange Bitters

__Glassware:__ Old Fashioned Glass

__Garnish:__ Orange twist, Brandied Cherry flag

__Instructions:__ _Shake with ice, strain, crushed ice, orange twist with brandied cherry_
