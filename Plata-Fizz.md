# Plata Fizz

* 2 oz Tequila Blanco
* 0.5 oz Cointreau
* 1 oz Lemon Juice
* 1 tsp Cinnamon Syrup
* 1 tsp Orgeat
* 0.5 oz Egg White
* Cream Soda (to top)

__Glassware:__ Fizz Glass

__Garnish:__ Orange twist

__Instructions:__ _Dry shake all but soda. Shake with ice. Strain into glass and top with cream soda. Garnish with orange zest._
